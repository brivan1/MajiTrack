Water Zones Management

Water Zones Management refers to the systematic approach of dividing a region into specific areas, known as Water Management Zones (WMZs), to effectively manage water resources. These zones are designed to address various aspects of water management, including surface water allocation, water quality, and activities within riverbeds and lakes. The primary goal is to integrate management practices across different ecosystems and activities within a catchment area.

"To ensure fair and responsive water zone management, MajiTrack provides tools for creating zone-specific rationing and supply strategies. This approach, which factors in individual zone requirements and water availability, promotes equitable distribution. Further, the system's ability to adjust rationing in real-time based on current data and forecasts means water allocation is always optimized and disruptions are minimized."
