OUR STORY

MajiTrack is a startup, that will harness the power of technology to enhance water accessibility and management for our users. Our dedicated team is focusing on delivering data-based tracking of water flow, ensuring that consumers have the information they need at their fingertips. We envision to partner with water utility providers to automate data collection, providing up-to-date insights into water distribution and usage. By facilitating direct communication between customers and utility services, we streamline management processes and simplify issue reporting. Join us in transforming how water is monitored and managed with MajiTrack.
